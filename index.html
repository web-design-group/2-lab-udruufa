MAIN CHARACTERS

Mabel Mora

Mabel Mora is part of the podcast trio. She is an artist who has lived in this building since childhood and she has her own secrets.

She is portrayed by Selena Gomez.

Charles-Haden Savage

Charles-Haden Savage is a semi-retired actor living in the Arconia, as well as a part of the newfound podcast trio.

He is portrayed by Steve Martin.

Oliver Putnam

Oliver Putnam is a struggling theater director and part of the podcast trio.

He is portrayed by Martin Short.
